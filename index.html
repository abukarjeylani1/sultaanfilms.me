Impact

Theatre & Cultural Impact

"Hoos" and "Date from Hell" were the historic films that reopened Mogadishu’s National Theatre after 30 years—featured in BBC, VOA News, and Arab News.

“It’s a platform that provides an opportunity for Somali songwriters, storytellers, movie directors and actors to present their talent openly.”
– Theatre Director Abdikadir Abdi Yusuf

We are proud to help restore Somali cinema and inspire dialogue through stories.
